Visualization section

  • A Guide to Who is Fighting Whom in Syria

    • This visualization is very clear and concise in describing the relationships between all those involved in the Syrian war. The use of colors also makes it easier to differentiate the relationships. Besides, being an interactive visualization it allows the viewer to get more information than what they can see at the first sight, by simply clicking on the faces. All in all, this simple and digestable visualisation provides a very comprehensive overview about the a complex issue like this which invloves expressing the relationships between multiple groups.

  • U.S. Wind Map

    • It is a good visualization to detect how the wind moves in the US. This simple and intuitive visualization makes it immediately clear for any kind of viewer what the general trends are without any need for numbers. Being an interactive visualisation, it also enables viewers to get more detailed information about the subject by simply clicking on different points on the map. A negative point about this visualization would be poor choice of colors describe the wind drection. Perhaps use of more contrasting colors could make the map easier to read and understand.
